Privacy Policy

CalcKit - Last updated: March 2026

Overview

CalcKit is a fully offline calculator app. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All calculations and history are stored locally on your device only.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No names, emails, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data is sent to any server

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is saved only on your device using Android's SharedPreferences and is never transmitted:

  • Your selected language preference
  • Your selected theme (light / dark)
  • Calculation history (last 30 entries)

Advertising (Google AdMob)

CalcKit displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain data to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers and usage data, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Children's Privacy

CalcKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
